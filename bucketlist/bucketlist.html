Bucket List
01

Climb Annapurna I

One of the deadliest mountains on Earth with a near 30% fatality rate. The danger is exactly what draws me to it.

02

Climb K2

Known as the Savage Mountain — it's considered even harder than Everest. Only the best climbers dare attempt it.

03

Climb Nanga Parbat

Called the Killer Mountain for a reason. It has claimed countless lives, but standing on its summit would be indescribable.

04

Climb Mount Everest

The roof of the world. Every climber's dream. I want to stand at 8,849m and see the curvature of the Earth.

05

Climb Eiger

Its infamous North Face is one of the great Alpine challenges. A mountain that has inspired climbers for generations.

06

Climb Mont Blanc

The highest peak in the Alps. A classic that every serious mountaineer should have on their list.

07

Do Skiing

I've always wanted to glide down a snow-covered slope. Ideally somewhere in the Swiss Alps.

08

Reach Challenger Rank in League of Legends

Top 300 players on the server. The absolute peak of ranked play. It's a grind, but I'll get there.

09

Own a Porsche 911 GT3

Naturally aspirated, rear-engined, and absolutely alive. The purist's sports car. My dream garage starts here.

10

Live for Good in Switzerland

Mountains, clean air, great food, and a peaceful life. Switzerland just feels like home even though I've never lived there.
