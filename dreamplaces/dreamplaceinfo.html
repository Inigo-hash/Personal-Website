🏔️ Switzerland

My #1 Dream Destination

Why I Want to Visit

My absolute dream travel destination has always been Switzerland. Beautiful lakes, lovely villages, and the towering Swiss Alps everything like scenes from a fairy tale. From the mountains covered in snow to the pristine waters of Lake Geneva and Lake Lucerne, the perfect mix of natural beauty and European elegance captivates me. I'm thrilled by the possibilities of traveling across mountain passes on scenic trains, seeing medieval villages, and taking in the Swiss way of life.

What I Want to See & Experience

  • Ride the Glacier Express and Bernina Express through the Alps
  • Visit the Matterhorn in Zermatt and Jungfraujoch - "Top of Europe"
  • Explore the stunning lakes: Lucerne, Geneva, and Brienz
  • Wander through charming towns like Interlaken, Grindelwald, and Lauterbrunnen
  • Taste authentic Swiss chocolate and fondue
  • Experience world-class skiing in the Swiss Alps

🌋 Iceland

Why I Want to Visit

Iceland feels like a different world, a place of ice and fire where the uncontrolled power of nature is on full show. Its striking scenery of volcanoes, geysers, glaciers, and black sand beaches is what draws me in. I deeply hope to have the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see the Northern Lights dancing across the Arctic sky. Iceland is unlike any other place on Earth because of its extraordinary beauty and unique geological features.

What I Want to See & Experience

  • Chase the Northern Lights (Aurora Borealis)
  • Visit stunning waterfalls: Gullfoss, Seljalandsfoss, Skógafoss
  • Walk on the black sand beaches of Reynisfjara
  • Relax in the Blue Lagoon geothermal spa
  • Explore ice caves and glacier lagoons like Jökulsárlón
  • See geysers erupting at Geysir geothermal area

❄️ Finland (Lapland)

Why I Want to Visit

The ultimate winter wonderland heaven is Finnish Lapland. The thought of being surrounded by immaculate snow-covered trees while lodging in a comfortable glass igloo or traditional log house is very fascinating. It's a dream come true to experience the husky sledding, the breathtaking arctic nights, and the potential to see the Aurora Borealis in this serene Arctic wilderness. Lapland is also the official home of Santa Claus.

What I Want to See & Experience

  • Stay in a glass igloo to watch the Northern Lights from bed
  • Go husky sledding through snowy forests
  • Meet reindeer and learn about Sami culture
  • Visit Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi
  • Try cross-country skiing and snowshoeing
  • Experience a traditional Finnish sauna

🧊 Greenland

Why I Want to Visit

The purest Arctic experience can be found in Greenland, a vast and intriguing region where glaciers stretch for miles and enormous icebergs float in beautiful waters. I'm fascinated by the remoteness and unspoiled beauty of this enormous island. It's even more alluring because it's one of the world's most isolated and unexplored locations. Its enormous ice sheet and lively Inuit culture provide a singular experience that can't be found anyplace else.

What I Want to See & Experience

  • Witness massive icebergs in Ilulissat Icefjord (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Take a boat tour among towering icebergs
  • See the Northern Lights in the Arctic darkness
  • Hike through Arctic tundra and glacial landscapes
  • Spot whales, seals, and Arctic wildlife
  • Visit colorful Inuit villages and learn about their culture

🏔️ Norway

Why I Want to Visit

Norway provides some of the most beautiful scenery in the world thanks to its dramatic fjords, tall mountains, and midnight sun. Norway is a nature lover's heaven because of its deep blue fjords sculpted by old glaciers, tumbling waterfalls, and charming communities nestled on mountain slopes. The thought of experiencing both the summer midnight sun and the enchanted snow-covered winter landscapes intrigues me.

What I Want to See & Experience

  • Cruise through the stunning Norwegian fjords (Geirangerfjord, Nærøyfjord)
  • Hike to iconic spots like Trolltunga and Preikestolen
  • See the Northern Lights in Tromsø
  • Ride the scenic Flåm Railway through mountains
  • Explore colorful Bergen and its historic Bryggen wharf
  • Experience the midnight sun above the Arctic Circle
